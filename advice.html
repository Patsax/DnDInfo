Advice

On this page, you will find links to Youtube channels that have useful videos on just about anything related to Dungeons and Dragons.

CMCC Builds

This channel does character builds. He also takes some of those builds and tests them out in Gauntlet style.

He has come up with some interesting builds, and his gauntlet videos are kind of fun.

d4: D&D Deep Dive

This channel does character builds. As he lays out the build, he also gives suggestions for character backstory and motivations.

He has a very soothing voice and the builds he comes up with can be very powerful.

DnD Shorts

As the name suggests, this channel has a lot of short videos that focus on a few tips and/or tricks for playing DnD.

Not typically my go-to source, but he has some good information.

Dungeon Dudes

This channel is more about tips and guidance for how to get the most fun out of DnD.

I've found them to be very useful for deciding which specific details to go with when making characters.

Treantmonk's Temple

This channel is all about taking very, very deep dives into the nitty-gritty of DnD.

While he does focus mainly on "rules as written" types of things, he does make a point of saying the rules are as flexible as your dm and table allow.

Tulok the Barbarian

This channel chock full of themed characters. For instance, if you wanted to make a DnD character based on a MCU character, chances are he already has a version figured out for you.
