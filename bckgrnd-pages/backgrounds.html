Character Backgrounds

Where a being starts often has a great influence on where that being goes.

Perhaps you are of noble birth, but for some reason your family name has been disgraced, and it now falls to you to restore its honor?

Perhaps your parents gave you up at an early age and now you just try to survive as a street urchin?

Are you somewhere between? Whatever the case may be, your background can have a profound affect upon you.

Your character's background is up to you, but these listings are good starting points. Each Background has a set of skills, languages, gear, and/or features that can be used in addition to what your character gets from their race and class.
